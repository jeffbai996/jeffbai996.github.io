About Praya Post

Established in 2011, Praya Post is the national postal service of the Republic of Praya, operating as a government agency funded by the Treasury. We're committed to providing reliable, affordable postal services to every community across our nation.

Services We Offer

  • Domestic parcel and package delivery
  • International shipping to 180+ countries
  • Post office box rentals at all locations
  • Registered mail with signature confirmation
  • Express and overnight delivery options
  • Business bulk mailing services
  • Packaging supplies and shipping materials

Coverage Area

Praya Post operates 124 post offices throughout the Republic, with service to every district including Braemar, Oakville, Metropolitan, Harbor District, and rural communities. International shipping available through partnerships with global postal unions.
